Our Story

Xavier Scent and Care was founded on the belief that luxury should be accessible. We curate the finest fragrances from around the globe, bringing them directly to your doorstep in Nigeria.

From the deep, resonant notes of Oud to the light, breezy touches of citrus, our collection is hand-picked to ensure longevity, projection, and elegance. We pride ourselves on authenticity and customer satisfaction.
